Bibliomantic: And LIS Blog

Bibliomantic is a blog for storing and sharing short-form writing for research in library and information science. This research fits into the context of an academic library at a small liberal arts college and from a professional perspective of an instructional services librarian.

What Kind of Writing Will You Find Here?

Library and information science represents a fairly large field with many divergent areas and specialties. While my current research interests are broad and not especially well-defined at the moment, there are several topic areas I'm currently interested in developing as a part of my professional activity:

  • Reference and Research Support
  • Critical Information Literacy
  • Critical Pedagogy
  • Theories of Knowledge
  • Information Behavior in the Everyday and Academic
  • Computer Mediated Information Seeking
  • Scholarly Communication
  • Knowledge Management Systems
  • History of Library Systems and Bibliographic Apparatus
  • Libraries as Institutions of Universal Education
